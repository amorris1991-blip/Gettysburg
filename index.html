⚔ Battle Rules

Remove an enemy unit if:

1. Your units are on opposite sides of it (N/S, E/W, or diagonal).

2. It borders 2+ of your units AND at least one of yours is on a hill tile.

🎴 True & False Rules

✓ Correct: You earn a Cannonade.

✗ Wrong: Your opponent earns the Cannonade instead as a penalty.

Each player answers privately on screen.

🏆 How to Win

Confederate wins:
Capture 3 ★ objectives, OR land 2 units on 2 objectives at once.

Union wins:
Survive all 12 rounds, or eliminate all Confederate units.
